Days Position Unfilled

On average, a professional position stays unfilled for approximately 90 days. This timeline includes:

  • Job posting and candidate sourcing
  • Screening and interview process
  • Final negotiations and onboarding

Understanding Salary Multiplier

The salary multiplier represents the value each employee generates for your organization:

  • 2x salary: Standard for most roles
  • 3x+ salary: High-performing/revenue-generating positions
  • 1.5-2x salary: Support or specialized roles

Each employee should ideally generate at least 2x their annual salary in value.
