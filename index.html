Days Position Unfilled

On average, a professional position stays unfilled for approximately 90 days. This includes:

  • Job posting and candidate sourcing
  • Screening and interview process
  • Negotiations and onboarding

Understanding Salary Multiplier

The salary multiplier represents the value each employee generates for your organization:

  • 2x salary: Standard for most roles (typical baseline)
  • 3x+ salary: High-performing/revenue-generating positions
  • 1-2x salary: Support or specialized roles

Historically, each employee should have a positive ROI of at least 2x their annual salary.
